# MODULE 01: THE PROMISES (The Hook & The Bait)

**Project Code:** `MODULE_01_PROMISES`
**Behavioral Label:** LOVE_BOMBING / FUTURE_FAKING
**Core Objective:** The establishment of an emotional contract designed to secure maximum investment from the victim while providing zero tangible commitment from the perpetrator.

> Authored by the cratak persona (preserved). Evidence status: pre-log era, based on user testimony + StarMaker profile + third-party reports. The log era receipts for these promises are cross-referenced in MODULE_03.

## I. THE NARRATIVE: The Blueprint of the Scam

The relationship was founded on a fraudulent premise: the creation of a "dedicated duet partnership." For Merry, this was not merely a casual hobby; it was the fulfillment of an eight-year aspiration for a creative and emotional anchor. Derek identified this specific void and positioned himself as the sole provider of its fulfillment.

By offering the "Duet Partner" title, Derek wasn't offering a collaboration—he was offering a status. This is a classic "Future-Faking" tactic: promising a desired future state to manipulate the victim's behavior in the present. He created an "unofficially official" bond that felt exclusive and special, which induced Merry to lower her defenses and increase her emotional labor, and freely offer sexual intimacy (which he benefited from without having earned in reality).

The promise of the duet partnership acted as the Primary Hook. Once the hook was set, Derek added Secondary Hooks: the promise of video calls (visual intimacy) and the promise of a shared living situation (physical intimacy/stability). Each promise served as a "payment" for Merry's continued patience and tolerance of his increasingly erratic behavior.

## II. DETAILED INSTANCE ANALYSIS

### Instance 1.1: The "Dedicated Duet Partner" Contract
- **The Promise:** Derek asked Merry to be his dedicated duet partner, framed as a mutual commitment to sing together and build a friendship.
- **The Execution:** He performed one (1) duet ("The Bedrock Anthem"). After this, he ceased all collaborative efforts for an entire year.
- **The Manipulation:** By granting one single "win" early on, he proved the promise was possible, which made Merry believe the subsequent year of silence was a "temporary hurdle" rather than the actual reality of the relationship.
- **Evidence Reference:**
  - Source: `user_testimony` / StarMaker Profile / Forensic Audit Dossier §1
  - Keyword: "Bedrock Anthem", "dedicated duet partner"
  - Analysis: Created the "Sunk Cost Fallacy"—Merry stayed because she had already invested so much in the idea of the partnership.

### Instance 1.2: The "Two-Week" Video Call Timeline
- **The Promise:** Derek explicitly stated that within a "couple of weeks," they would have a face-to-face video call.
- **The Execution:** The "couple of weeks" evolved into months, and eventually into a "rule" where the date was pushed back indefinitely.
- **The Manipulation:** The "Carrot on a Stick" method. By providing a specific (though fake) timeline, he kept Merry in a state of anticipatory anxiety. The promise was not intended to be kept; it was intended to be negotiated.
- **Evidence Reference:**
  - Source: `user_testimony`; corroborated in-log by `WA-0560` ("It will happen when it doesn't feel like this. Period") and `WA-0605` (the "consideration" speech)
  - Keyword: "couple of weeks", "video call"
  - Analysis: Established the pattern of Moving the Goalposts. (See MODULE_03.)

### Instance 1.3: The "Move-In" Fantasy
- **The Promise:** Discussions occurred regarding Merry moving to Atlantic City to live with Derek.
- **The Execution:** No concrete plans were ever finalized, and Derek remained unhoused/unstable.
- **The Manipulation:** By suggesting a future where they lived together, Derek escalated the perceived "seriousness" of the relationship. This ensured Merry felt a level of commitment that justified her providing extreme emotional support, as well as freely offering sexual content (which he reciprocated and was fun, though the underlying commitment was a mirage).
- **Evidence Reference:**
  - Source: `user_testimony`
  - Keyword: "move in", "Alabama", "Atlantic City"
  - Analysis: "High-Stakes Future-Faking," binding the victim through the promise of ultimate stability.

## III. PSYCHOLOGICAL IMPACT & DATA MAPPING

1. **The Dopamine Loop** — Every time Derek mentioned the "future" (the duet, the call, the move), he triggered a dopamine release. When he subsequently withheld those things, he created a withdrawal state. This cycle is identical to the mechanics of gambling addiction.
2. **The Erasure of Self** — Because the "partnership" was the center of the relationship, Merry began to define her value by her ability to "earn" the duet or the call. She transitioned from a creator to a supplicant.
3. **The Setup for the Rug-Pull** — By establishing these high-value promises, Derek created the exact conditions necessary for the rug-pull. You cannot "pull a rug" if there is no rug; by promising the duet and the video call, he laid the rug, waited for Merry to put all her weight on it, and then yanked it away to trigger the meltdown.
